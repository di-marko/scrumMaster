What is Agile? What is Scrum?

Agile is a mindset: a set of values and principles. These values and principles are presented in the Agile Manifesto .

Scrum is a way in which this mindset can be applied. It is an iterative approach where a team of 4-6 people regularly deliver a working product (feature) that meets the user's stories.

You can say that Scrum is Agile, however, Agile doesn't have to be Scrum.

Roles

Product Owner

This person closely works with the stakeholders and customers. He or she decides WHAT needs to be worked on and WHY it needs to be worked on.

Scrum Master

This person is responsible for the whole Scrum process to be run smoothly and incrementally.

Development Team

Based on the Product Owner's priority list the development team developes the product. The work is planned within the team and all progress is tracked for future improvements.
